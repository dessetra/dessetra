# Reflection

Many participants focus on finding the next opportunity.

Fewer focus on protecting themselves.

Before entering any DeFi protocol, ask:

- Do I understand the risks?
- What could go wrong?
- How much can I afford to lose?
- Am I making this decision emotionally or logically?

Long-term success often comes from avoiding major mistakes rather than chasing the biggest rewards.

Protecting capital is one of the most valuable skills in finance.
