# Reflection

Many people first hear about DeFi through stories of profits and rewards.

However, successful participation starts with understanding.

Ask yourself:

- Do I understand how the system works?
- Do I understand where rewards come from?
- Do I understand what risks exist?
- Am I learning before participating?

The goal is not simply to earn.

The goal is to make informed decisions.

Knowledge before participation is one of the most valuable habits in Web3.
