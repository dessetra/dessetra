# Reflection

Liquidity pools and yield farming can look attractive because of the rewards they offer.

But rewards should never be viewed alone.

Before providing liquidity, ask:

- Do I understand the assets in the pool?
- Do I understand impermanent loss?
- Do I understand the smart contract or platform risk?
- Am I chasing rewards without understanding the danger?

DeFi rewards often exist because someone is accepting risk.

A careful participant studies the risk before entering the pool.
