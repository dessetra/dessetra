# DeFi & Passive Income

You now understand how to participate more safely in Web3.

You know about tokens, stablecoins, exchanges, wallet addresses, networks, and scams.

Now it is time to understand one of the biggest reasons people enter Web3:

> Decentralized Finance.

DeFi allows people to interact with financial tools without depending completely on traditional banks.

People can:

- Stake assets
- Lend assets
- Borrow assets
- Provide liquidity
- Earn rewards

But rewards always come with risks.

In this module, you will learn how DeFi works and how to think carefully before participating.

The goal is not to chase income blindly.

The goal is to understand how Web3 income systems work before using them.
