# Reflection

Many people are attracted to staking because of the rewards.

However, responsible participation requires looking beyond percentages.

Before staking any asset, consider:

- What risks am I taking?
- How long will the asset be locked?
- What happens if the token price falls?
- Do I understand how the staking system works?

Successful participants focus on understanding before committing capital.

The highest reward is not always the best opportunity.
