# Reflection

Lending and borrowing can create flexibility within a financial system.

However, every loan introduces responsibility and risk.

Before borrowing, ask yourself:

- Do I understand the collateral requirements?
- What happens if the market moves against me?
- Could my position be liquidated?
- Am I borrowing for a clear purpose?

Before lending, ask:

- Do I trust the protocol?
- Do I understand the risks?
- Am I comfortable with potential losses?

Smart participation begins with understanding the rules before committing assets.
