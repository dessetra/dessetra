# Mindset Before Money

Many people enter Web3 with one goal:

> Make money quickly.

Unfortunately, this mindset often leads to poor decisions.

People chase hype.

People ignore risks.

People follow strangers online.

People invest in things they do not understand.

The result is often disappointment.

## Why Mindset Matters

Your mindset influences every decision you make.

A person focused only on fast profits may:

- Take unnecessary risks
- Ignore warning signs
- Panic during downturns
- Jump from project to project

A person focused on learning and growth is more likely to:

- Make informed decisions
- Remain patient
- Manage risk
- Build long-term success

## Wealth Is Usually Built Slowly

Social media often highlights:

- Overnight success stories
- Massive gains
- Luxury lifestyles

What is rarely shown is:

- Years of learning
- Mistakes and failures
- Patience and discipline
- Consistent effort

Long-term success is usually built gradually.

## Focus On Skills

Money can come and go.

Skills often remain valuable.

Examples include:

- Research
- Communication
- Community building
- Content creation
- Technical knowledge
- Problem solving

The more useful your skills become, the more opportunities you can create.

## Avoid Comparison

Everyone's journey is different.

Comparing yourself constantly to others can lead to:

- Frustration
- Poor decisions
- Unnecessary risk taking

Focus on your own progress.

## Think Long Term

Ask yourself:

- Where do I want to be in three years?
- What skills am I developing?
- What habits am I building?
- Am I becoming more knowledgeable?

Long-term thinking often produces better outcomes than short-term excitement.

## Dessetra Insight

Opportunities come and go.

A strong mindset allows you to recognize opportunities, avoid mistakes, and continue growing regardless of market conditions.

Success begins with how you think before it appears in your wallet.
