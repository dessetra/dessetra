# The Awakening

For years, people have used the internet without truly owning anything.

Your accounts, your content, your audience, your money, and even your identity have mostly been controlled by centralized platforms.

Then something began to change.

People around the world started hearing new words:
- Bitcoin
- Blockchain
- Crypto
- Web3
- Decentralization

Some became curious.
Some became afraid.
Some rushed in blindly and lost money.
Others quietly took time to understand.

Dessetra was created for those who want clarity before confusion, wisdom before hype, and understanding before risk.

This is not just about making money.

This is about learning how the digital world is changing — and discovering how to navigate it intelligently and safely.

Welcome to your awakening.
