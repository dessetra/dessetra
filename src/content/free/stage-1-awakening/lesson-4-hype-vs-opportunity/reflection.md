# Reflection

Hype becomes dangerous when people stop thinking clearly.

Most bad decisions in Web3 do not begin with technology.

They begin with emotions:
- fear,
- greed,
- urgency,
- pressure,
- and comparison.

## Think About This

Have you ever felt pressured to join something online because it looked like everyone else was already benefiting from it?

Before moving forward, write down one rule you want to follow before trusting any Web3 opportunity.

> The calm learner often sees what the excited crowd ignores.
