# Reflection

Technology keeps evolving, whether people pay attention or not.

Some people adapt early.
Others ignore change until it affects them directly.

## Think About This

How much of your current digital life depends on platforms you do not control?

Consider:
- your social media accounts
- your online business
- your communication channels
- your financial tools

If one platform disappeared tomorrow, what would happen to your digital presence?

Understanding change early creates awareness before dependence becomes a problem.

> Intelligent participation begins with awareness.
