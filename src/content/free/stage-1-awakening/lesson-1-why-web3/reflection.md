# Reflection

Before moving forward, pause for a moment.

Most people enter Web3 because they hear about money.

But real understanding begins when you ask better questions.

## Think About This

What part of your current digital life do you feel you do not truly control?

It could be:
- your social media account
- your online audience
- your money
- your personal data
- your digital identity

Write down one thing you want to understand better as you continue this journey.

> Clarity is the first step toward intelligent participation.
