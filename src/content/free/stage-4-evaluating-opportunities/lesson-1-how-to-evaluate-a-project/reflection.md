# Reflection

Many people discover projects because of excitement.

However, excitement should be the beginning of research, not the end of it.

Before evaluating any opportunity, ask:

- What problem does this project solve?
- Is there a real product?
- Who benefits from the project?
- Is the team transparent?
- Am I making a decision based on facts or emotions?

Strong opportunities can withstand questions.

Weak opportunities often depend on hype.
