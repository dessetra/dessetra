# Reflection

Many poor decisions begin with emotion rather than understanding.

Before participating in any opportunity, ask yourself:

- Am I feeling pressured?
- Am I acting because of hype?
- Have I reviewed the risks?
- Is there a real product?
- Would I still be interested if nobody was talking about it?

Good opportunities can survive scrutiny.

Bad opportunities often depend on urgency, emotion, and unrealistic promises.

Learning to recognize red flags is one of the most valuable skills in Web3.
