# Reflection

Many people focus on a token's price.

However, understanding tokenomics provides a deeper perspective.

Before evaluating a token, ask:

- What purpose does the token serve?
- How many tokens exist?
- How are tokens distributed?
- Is the supply increasing over time?
- Does demand support the token's design?

Strong investors often study tokenomics before considering price movements.

Understanding the system behind a token can help avoid emotional decisions.
