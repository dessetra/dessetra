# Reflection

Many people evaluate projects based only on price.

However, long-term success is often influenced by people and utility.

Before supporting a project, ask:

- Is the team actively building?
- Does the community provide value?
- Does the project solve a real problem?
- Would people use this even if the token price was not increasing?

Strong opportunities often have strong fundamentals.

Price may attract attention, but utility sustains growth.
