# Evaluating Web3 Opportunities

By now, you understand the foundations of Web3 and how DeFi systems operate.

But knowledge of technology alone is not enough.

Every day, thousands of projects compete for attention.

Some are legitimate.

Some are poorly designed.

Some are outright scams.

The challenge is not finding opportunities.

The challenge is identifying quality opportunities.

In this module, you will learn how experienced participants evaluate projects before committing time, attention, or capital.

You will learn:

- How to analyze a project
- How tokenomics influence value
- Why teams and communities matter
- How to spot warning signs
- How to build your own evaluation framework

The goal is not to predict the future.

The goal is to make better decisions with the information available.
