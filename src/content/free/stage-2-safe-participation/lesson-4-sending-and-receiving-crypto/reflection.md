# Reflection

Many Web3 losses happen because of simple mistakes rather than complicated scams.

A wrong wallet address.

A wrong network.

A rushed transaction.

The good news is that most of these mistakes are preventable.

As you continue your Web3 journey, remember:

- Slow down before confirming transactions.
- Verify wallet addresses carefully.
- Confirm the correct network.
- Never let urgency replace accuracy.

A few extra seconds of verification can save you from permanent loss.
