# Reflection

Web3 safety is not only about technology.

It is also about discipline.

Many scams succeed because people act too quickly, trust too easily, or ignore warning signs.

Before trusting any opportunity, ask yourself:

- Is someone creating pressure?
- Am I being promised guaranteed profits?
- Am I being asked for private information?
- Have I verified the source?
- Would I still make this decision if I slowed down?

A safe Web3 participant does not rush.

They verify, think, and protect themselves before acting.
