# Reflection

Before this lesson, many digital assets may have seemed identical.

Now you know that not all crypto assets serve the same purpose.

Some are coins that power entire networks.

Others are tokens built on existing blockchains.

Before buying any digital asset, take a moment to ask:

- What is this asset?
- What purpose does it serve?
- Which network does it use?
- Why do people use it?

Understanding these questions can help you avoid costly mistakes and become a more confident participant in Web3.
