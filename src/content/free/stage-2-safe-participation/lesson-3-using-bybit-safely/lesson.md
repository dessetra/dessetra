# Using Bybit Safely

Learning about Web3 is important.

But eventually, most people will need a way to obtain digital assets such as USDT.

One of the most popular ways to do this is through a cryptocurrency exchange.

For this lesson, we will use Bybit as our example.

## What Is Bybit?

Bybit is a centralized cryptocurrency exchange.

This means it provides a platform where users can:

* Buy crypto assets
* Sell crypto assets
* Deposit funds
* Withdraw funds
* Convert between digital assets

For many beginners, an exchange acts as the bridge between traditional money and Web3.

## Creating An Account

To use Bybit, you first need:

* An email address
  or
* A mobile phone number

Always use information that you control and can access.

Avoid using temporary email addresses.

## Identity Verification (KYC)

Bybit may require identity verification.

This process is often called:

> Know Your Customer (KYC)

Verification helps:

* Protect users
* Reduce fraud
* Improve account security
* Unlock additional platform features

## Security Comes First

Before depositing any funds, activate:

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

2FA adds an extra layer of protection to your account.

Even if someone learns your password, they will still need the second authentication method.

This is one of the most important security steps every user should take.

## Purchasing USDT

Many beginners purchase USDT first because:

* It is widely accepted
* It is easier to understand than volatile assets
* It can be transferred to wallets and other platforms

Always double-check:

* The amount
* The payment method
* The network being used

## Important Reminder

Never share:

* Your password
* Verification codes
* Authentication codes

No legitimate support agent will ever ask for them.

## Dessetra Insight

A secure account is more valuable than a profitable trade.

Before focusing on opportunities, focus on protecting your access.
