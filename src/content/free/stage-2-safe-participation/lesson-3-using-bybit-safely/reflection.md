# Reflection

A cryptocurrency exchange is often the first place where people interact with Web3.

Because exchanges hold access to your funds, security should always be your highest priority.

As you continue your journey, remember:

- Strong passwords matter.
- Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) matters.
- Verification codes should never be shared.
- Protecting your account is more important than chasing quick profits.

A secure account gives you the confidence to participate safely in the Web3 economy.
