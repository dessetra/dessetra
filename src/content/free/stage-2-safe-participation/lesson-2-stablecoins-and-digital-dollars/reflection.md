# Reflection

Stablecoins play an important role in helping people participate in Web3 with greater confidence.

Unlike many crypto assets that experience significant price fluctuations, stablecoins are designed to maintain a relatively stable value.

As you continue your Web3 journey, remember:

- Not every digital asset behaves the same way.
- Stablecoins can be useful for payments and transfers.
- Choosing the correct network is just as important as choosing the correct asset.
- Security and verification should always come before convenience.

A knowledgeable participant focuses on understanding how assets work before using them.
