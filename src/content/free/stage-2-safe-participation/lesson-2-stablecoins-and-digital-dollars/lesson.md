# Stablecoins & Digital Dollars

One of the biggest challenges in cryptocurrency is price volatility.

An asset may be worth $100 today and $80 tomorrow.

For beginners, this can be confusing and risky.

This is where stablecoins become important.

## What Is A Stablecoin?

A stablecoin is a digital asset designed to maintain a relatively stable value.

Most stablecoins aim to remain close to:

> 1 Stablecoin = 1 US Dollar

This makes them easier to use for payments, savings, and transactions.

## Common Stablecoins

Some popular stablecoins include:

* USDT (Tether)
* USDC (USD Coin)

These are widely used throughout the Web3 ecosystem.

## Why People Use Stablecoins

People use stablecoins because they:

* Reduce volatility
* Make transactions easier
* Allow users to store value digitally
* Simplify international transfers

Many Web3 participants move their profits into stablecoins when markets become uncertain.

## Why Dessetra Uses USDT

Throughout the Dessetra ecosystem, USDT is the primary digital dollar used for:

* Payments
* Rewards
* Future subscriptions
* Future commission withdrawals

USDT is familiar to most Web3 users and is supported across many platforms.

## Important Reminder

Stablecoins are useful, but they are not risk-free.

Always:

* Verify the network you are using
* Double-check wallet addresses
* Use trusted platforms
* Protect your account security
