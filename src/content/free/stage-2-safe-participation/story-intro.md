# Safe Participation

You now understand what Web3 is and why it matters.

But knowledge alone is not enough.

Many people enter Web3 excited about opportunities but lose money because they do not understand the tools.

They buy the wrong assets.

They use the wrong networks.

They trust the wrong people.

They click the wrong links.

In this stage, you will learn how to participate safely.

You will learn:

- What crypto tokens are
- Why stablecoins matter
- How exchanges work
- How to move digital assets safely
- How to avoid common scams

The goal is not speed.

The goal is confidence.

A smart participant survives longer than an excited participant.
